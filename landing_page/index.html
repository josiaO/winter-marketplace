Escrow ni nini?

Escrow ni kama “sanduku la usalama” lililopo katikati ya mnunuzi na muuzaji. Inahakikisha kuwa kila mtu anapata anachostahili kabla ya pesa kubadilishana mikono.

Inavyofanya kazi:

Mnunuzi analipa: Pesa haitumiwi muuzaji moja kwa moja, bali inaingia kwenye mfumo wetu salama.

Pesa inashikiliwa: SmartDalali inaweza kuiona pesa hiyo kwenye mfumo, lakini haiwezi kutumika vibaya. Imetulia hapo kusubiri biashara imalizike.

Bidhaa inatumwa: Muuzaji anatuma bidhaa akijua fika kuwa pesa ipo tayari.

Malipo yanatolewa: Mnunuzi akipokea na kuridhika, mfumo unaachia pesa na kumpatia muuzaji.

Uhakika wa 100%: Mfumo umeundwa ili pesa isitolewe bila kufuata hatua za escrow — kazi ya SmartDalali ni kuhakikisha usalama wa pande zote mbili mpaka biashara itakapotimia.

Kwa nini mfumo huu ni bora?

Kwa Mnunuzi: Hutopoteza pesa kwa tapeli; usipopata bidhaa, unarudishiwa pesa yako.

Kwa Muuzaji: Una uhakika kuwa mteja ana pesa, na utalipwa mara tu atakapopokea mzigo.

Tembelea sehemu ya escrow kwenye ukurasa →

Escrow is like a “safe box” between buyer and seller. It makes sure each side gets what they deserve before money changes hands.

How it works:

Buyer pays: Money does not go straight to the seller; it enters our secure system first.

Funds are held: I (the marketplace) can see that money, but I cannot touch or spend it. It stays there until the deal is finished.

Item is shipped: The seller ships knowing the funds are in place.

Payment is released: When the buyer receives the item and is happy, the system releases the money to the seller.

100% assurance: Even I as the marketplace owner cannot take your money. My job is only to oversee the process and keep both sides safe until the transaction is complete.

Why this system is better:

For buyers: You do not lose money to fraud; if you do not get the item, you get your money back.

For sellers: You know the customer has paid, and you get paid once they receive the shipment.
